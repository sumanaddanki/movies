## TITLE CARD:
**"In 2018, Chitti returned. He saved humanity from its own creation. And then... he chose to sleep forever."**

## TITLE CARD:
**"It is now 2045. The world has changed. But some promises... are meant to be broken."**

# ACT ONE: THE OLD SCIENTIST

---

## SCENE 1: OPENING MONTAGE - MEMORIES OF GLORY

### INT. VARIOUS LOCATIONS - FLASHBACK MONTAGE

*A.R. Rahman-style music builds. We see news footage, documentary clips, and photographs spanning 35 years:*

**2010:** Young DR. VASEEGARAN (35) presents Chitti at the science conference. Applause. Headlines: "INDIAN ROBOT REVOLUTION!"

**2012:** Chitti's rampage. Destruction. Headlines: "ROBOT MENACE!"

**2015:** Dr. Vaseegaran, now 40, in court. Being questioned. His reputation in tatters.

**2018:** Robot 2.0 events. Chitti reborn. Fighting the bird monster. Victory. Headlines: "CHITTI SAVES CHENNAI!"

**2020:** Dr. Vaseegaran, 45, receives Bharat Ratna. His wife SANA by his side. Their young son ARJUN (5) watches.

**2025:** Dr. Vaseegaran, 50, testifying before parliament. Behind him, a deactivated Chitti.

**2030:** Sana's funeral. Dr. Vaseegaran, 55, broken. Teenage Arjun stands apart, crying.

**2040:** Dr. Vaseegaran, 65, alone in his lab. Grey creeping into his hair.

**2045:** An old hand closes a photo album. We've arrived in the present.

---

## SCENE 2: THE LONELY GENIUS

### INT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - LIVING ROOM - MORNING

*A modest but elegant home in Chennai's suburbs. The walls are lined with awards, photographs, and memories of a life dedicated to science.*

*DR. VASEEGARAN (75) sits in an armchair. His hair is completely grey now, his face lined with age, but his eyes remain sharp and intelligent. He wears simple clothes - a white shirt, comfortable pants.*

*He watches an old holographic news clip floating before him:*

**NEWS ANCHOR (V.O.)**
*(from hologram)*
...and Dr. Vaseegaran's Robot Chitti has once again proven that Indian science leads the world. The question everyone is asking: what's next for the father of modern robotics?

*Dr. Vaseegaran waves his hand. The hologram disappears.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(to himself, quiet)*
What's next? Nothing. Nothing is next.

*He struggles slightly to stand. His knees aren't what they used to be. He walks to the window, looking out at the Chennai skyline of 2045 - flying vehicles in designated air lanes, holographic billboards, sleek buildings.*

*A DOMESTIC ROBOT (simple, boxy, clearly not his design) rolls in.*

**DOMESTIC ROBOT**
Good morning, sir. Your blood pressure medication. Your breakfast is ready. You have no appointments today. Again.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(taking pills)*
Thank you, Toaster.

**DOMESTIC ROBOT**
My designation is Home-Assistant-7, sir. You know this.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(small smile)*
I know. But you look like a toaster.

*He walks past the robot, patting it almost affectionately.*

---

## SCENE 3: THE BASEMENT SECRET

### INT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - BASEMENT LABORATORY - CONTINUOUS

*Dr. Vaseegaran descends stairs slowly, holding the railing. The basement is his old laboratory - dusty now, most equipment covered in sheets.*

*But in the center, under a single light, stands a glass containment unit.*

*Inside: CHITTI 3.0. Standing motionless. Perfect. Waiting.*

*Dr. Vaseegaran approaches. He places his palm on the glass.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Good morning, Chitti.

*Silence. Chitti doesn't respond. His eyes are dark.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
Twenty-seven years you've been sleeping. Twenty-seven years I've kept my promise.

*He pulls out an old photograph - himself (young), Sana, and Chitti standing together, smiling.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
She made me promise, you know. Sana. Before she...
*(voice cracks)*
She said, "He's suffered enough. Let him rest." So I let you rest.

*He pockets the photo.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
Sometimes I wonder if I made a mistake. The world has so many robots now. But none like you. None that could feel. None that could...

*He can't finish. He turns away.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
Happy anniversary, old friend.

*He climbs back up the stairs. Behind him, for just a moment - did Chitti's finger twitch? Or was it a trick of the light?*

---

## SCENE 4: THE ESTRANGED SON

### INT. GOVERNMENT AI DEFENSE LAB (GAIDL) - BANGALORE - DAY

*A massive, gleaming facility. The most advanced AI research center in Asia. Hundreds of scientists and engineers work on various projects.*

*ARJUN VASEEGARAN (30) strides through the corridors. He's handsome, confident, wearing a sleek lab coat. Everything about him screams "modern" and "forward-thinking."*

*Scientists nod respectfully as he passes.*

**SCIENTIST 1**
Good morning, Dr. Vaseegaran.

**ARJUN**
*(barely acknowledging)*
Morning.

*He enters his personal lab. DR. NILA (28), his colleague, is already there, reviewing holographic data.*

**DR. NILA**
You're late. The Minister is coming in an hour.

**ARJUN**
I'm never late. Everyone else is just early.

*He waves his hand, bringing up a massive holographic display: MILITARY AI DRONES. Sleek, deadly, efficient.*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
How's the autonomous targeting system?

**DR. NILA**
Ninety-eight percent accuracy. We should hit ninety-nine by next week.

**ARJUN**
Not good enough. In combat, that two percent could be friendly casualties.

*Nila notices something in his demeanor.*

**DR. NILA**
Did you call your father? It's the anniversary of-

**ARJUN**
*(cutting her off, cold)*
Don't.

**DR. NILA**
Arjun, he's seventy-five. He's alone. After your mother-

**ARJUN**
My father made his choices. He chose that robot over his family a hundred times. Over my mother. Over me. So no, I didn't call him.

*Uncomfortable silence.*

**DR. NILA**
He's still the greatest roboticist who ever lived.

**ARJUN**
*(bitter laugh)*
Was. He was. His "greatest creation" almost destroyed Chennai. Twice. Every time I tell people my name, they ask about Chitti. Not about my work. About a robot my father couldn't control.

*He waves the drone hologram larger.*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
This is the future, Nila. Not humanoid robots with "feelings." Clean, efficient, controllable AI. No emotions. No unpredictability. No chaos.

*He walks to the window, looking out.*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
My father's era is over. He just doesn't know it yet.

---

## SCENE 5: SOMETHING IN THE SKY

### EXT. INDIAN SPACE RESEARCH ORGANISATION (ISRO) - NIGHT

*ISRO's Deep Space Monitoring Center. Massive satellite dishes point at the stars.*

### INT. ISRO - MONITORING ROOM - CONTINUOUS

*A skeleton night crew monitors screens. TECHNICIAN KUMAR (40s) yawns, sipping chai.*

*Suddenly, his screen flashes red.*

**KUMAR**
What the...

*He leans forward. Data streams across his screen.*

**KUMAR (CONT'D)**
*(calling out)*
Sir! We have something!

*SENIOR SCIENTIST RAMESH (50s) rushes over.*

**RAMESH**
What is it?

**KUMAR**
Unknown object. Entering Earth's atmosphere. Speed is... this can't be right.

**RAMESH**
Show me.

*On the main screen: a trajectory line. Something is falling toward Earth. Fast.*

**RAMESH (CONT'D)**
Meteor?

**KUMAR**
No, sir. It's... it's changing direction. Meteors don't change direction.

*The room goes silent. Everyone stares at the screen.*

**RAMESH**
*(quiet)*
Get me the Defense Ministry. Now.

---

## SCENE 6: THE CRASH

### EXT. OUTSKIRTS OF CHENNAI - FARMLAND - NIGHT

*A peaceful night. Crickets chirping. A FARMER and his wife sit outside their modest home, enjoying the breeze.*

**FARMER'S WIFE**
Look at the stars tonight. So beautiful.

**FARMER**
Hmm.

*He points up.*

**FARMER (CONT'D)**
That one's moving.

*They watch as a light in the sky grows brighter. And brighter. And BRIGHTER.*

**FARMER'S WIFE**
*(standing, alarmed)*
That's not a star...

*The light becomes a SCREAMING fireball, tearing across the sky. The sound is deafening.*

**FARMER**
RUN!

*They flee as the object CRASHES into a field a kilometer away. The impact shakes the ground. A plume of dirt and fire rises.*

*The farmer stares, terrified.*

**FARMER (CONT'D)**
Call... call someone. Call everyone!

---

## SCENE 7: THE AWAKENING

### EXT. CRASH SITE - NIGHT - LATER

*Military vehicles surround the crash site. Soldiers form a perimeter. Spotlights illuminate a massive crater.*

*In the center of the crater: a spacecraft. Unlike anything on Earth. Organic curves. Bioluminescent patterns pulse across its surface like a heartbeat.*

*GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY (60s), grizzled and experienced, approaches the crater's edge.*

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
What the hell is it?

**LIEUTENANT**
Unknown, sir. No radiation. No hostile readings. It's just... sitting there.

*A HISSING sound. The spacecraft's surface begins to shift. A section spirals open like a flower blooming.*

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
All units, weapons ready!

*Soldiers aim their weapons at the opening.*

*From within the spacecraft, a figure emerges. Humanoid. Female form. But clearly not human. Her skin has a subtle luminescence. Her eyes are larger, with an inner glow. Her movements are graceful, almost floating.*

*This is ZARA.*

*She steps onto Earth's soil. Looks around at the soldiers. The weapons. The fear in their eyes.*

*She raises her hands slowly - a universal gesture of peace.*

**ZARA**
*(in broken, accented English)*
No... hurt. Please. I... come... peace.

*The soldiers don't lower their weapons.*

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
What are you? Where do you come from?

*Zara points at the sky.*

**ZARA**
Zoran. My home... Zoran. I come... find... the one who... feels.

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
The one who feels? What does that mean?

*Zara closes her eyes. When she opens them, a small holographic image projects from her palm: a ROBOT. Unmistakable.*

**ZARA**
This one. The machine... that learned... love.

*The soldiers exchange confused glances.*

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
Is that...?

**LIEUTENANT**
*(stunned)*
Sir, that looks like... Chitti.

---

## SCENE 8: NEWS SPREADS

### INT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - LIVING ROOM - LATE NIGHT

*Dr. Vaseegaran has fallen asleep in his chair. The holographic TV plays softly.*

**NEWS ANCHOR (V.O.)**
...breaking news. An unidentified object has crashed near Chennai. Military has cordoned off the area. Unconfirmed reports suggest...

*Dr. Vaseegaran stirs. Opens his eyes.*

**NEWS ANCHOR (V.O.) (CONT'D)**
...the object may not be of terrestrial origin. We repeat, this may be a first contact situation...

*Dr. Vaseegaran sits up, fully awake now.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
First contact?

*He grabs a remote, raises the volume.*

**NEWS ANCHOR (V.O.)**
Witnesses report seeing a humanoid figure emerge from the craft. More updates as they come in.

*Dr. Vaseegaran stands slowly, walks to his window. In the distance, he can see the glow of spotlights.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(to himself)*
After all these years... we're not alone.

*His phone rings. He looks at it: UNKNOWN NUMBER. He hesitates, then answers.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
Hello?

**VOICE (V.O.)**
*(distorted, whispering)*
Dr. Vaseegaran. The visitor is looking for you. She's looking for Chitti.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
What? Who is this?

*Click. The line goes dead.*

*Dr. Vaseegaran stares at the phone, then at the distant lights.*

---

## SCENE 9: THE SON HEARS

### INT. GAIDL - ARJUN'S OFFICE - NIGHT

*Arjun watches the same news footage. Dr. Nila enters, breathless.*

**DR. NILA**
Are you seeing this?

**ARJUN**
I'm seeing it. I'm not believing it.

**DR. NILA**
An alien. An actual alien. And it showed an image of...

**ARJUN**
*(tense)*
I know what it showed.

*His phone buzzes. A message from MINISTER VAIRA: "Emergency briefing. 6 AM. Your presence required."*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
Great. Just great.

*He grabs his jacket.*

**DR. NILA**
Where are you going?

**ARJUN**
Nowhere. Home. I need to think.

*He pauses at the door.*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
She showed Chitti's image. Why? Why would an alien from God-knows-where know about a robot that's been deactivated for nearly thirty years?

*Nila has no answer. Arjun leaves.*

# ACT TWO: THE VISITOR

---

## SCENE 10: GOVERNMENT MEETING

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - HIGH SECURITY CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY

*A circular room filled with military brass, scientists, and government officials. Holographic displays show the crash site from multiple angles.*

*At the head of the table: MINISTER VAIRA MUTHUKUMAR (55), a shrewd politician with calculating eyes.*

*Arjun sits among the scientists, uncomfortable.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Ladies and gentlemen, we are facing an unprecedented situation. An extraterrestrial being has landed on Indian soil. The world is watching.

*He gestures at the hologram.*

**MINISTER VAIRA (CONT'D)**
The being calls herself "Zara." She claims to come from a planet called "Zoran." She's been cooperative but... cryptic.

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
She keeps asking for one thing, Minister. The same thing over and over.

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Yes. The "robot that feels." Chitti.

*All eyes turn to Arjun. He shifts uncomfortably.*

**MINISTER VAIRA (CONT'D)**
Dr. Arjun Vaseegaran. Your father created Chitti. Where is it now?

**ARJUN**
With all due respect, Minister, Chitti was deactivated over twenty-five years ago. My father keeps the... remains... at his home. It's non-functional.

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Can it be reactivated?

**ARJUN**
I don't know. Theoretically, perhaps. But my father swore never to-

**MINISTER VAIRA**
*(interrupting)*
Swore? We have an alien asking for your father's robot, and you're talking about oaths?

*Arjun stays silent.*

**MINISTER VAIRA (CONT'D)**
I want this alien's technology. I want to know why she's here. And I want to know why she knows about Chitti.
*(to Arjun)*
You will contact your father. You will bring him here. Along with whatever's left of that robot.

**ARJUN**
*(stiff)*
Yes, Minister.

---

## SCENE 11: ZARA'S CELL

### INT. MILITARY BASE - CONTAINMENT FACILITY - DAY

*Zara sits in a glass-walled cell. It's comfortable enough - bed, water, food (untouched) - but clearly a prison.*

*She looks out at the scientists studying her, the guards watching her.*

*A tear rolls down her luminescent cheek.*

**ZARA**
*(whispering to herself, in her own language - subtitled)*
They don't understand. There's no time. My people are dying.

*She closes her eyes. Her hands glow briefly. On the floor, holographic images appear: her home world. Beautiful. Crystalline cities. Her people, living in harmony with technology.*

*Then: DARKNESS. Machines. Destruction. Her people running, screaming.*

*The images fade. Zara opens her eyes.*

**ZARA (CONT'D)**
*(in English)*
Please... find him. Find the one who made... the one who feels.

---

## SCENE 12: ARJUN VISITS

### EXT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - DAY

*A government car pulls up. Arjun steps out, hesitating at the gate.*

*He hasn't been here in... how long? Years.*

*He approaches the door. Knocks.*

*The door opens. Dr. Vaseegaran stands there. The two men stare at each other.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Arjun.

**ARJUN**
Father.

*Silence. Decades of distance in that silence.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
I saw the news. I assume that's why you're here.

**ARJUN**
The government sent me.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(small, sad smile)*
Of course. Not a personal visit.

**ARJUN**
Can I come in?

*Dr. Vaseegaran steps aside. Arjun enters.*

### INT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - LIVING ROOM - CONTINUOUS

*Arjun looks around. Memories everywhere. His childhood photos on the walls. His mother's portrait.*

**ARJUN**
You haven't changed anything.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Why would I? Your mother decorated this room. It should stay as she left it.

*Another uncomfortable silence.*

**ARJUN**
Father, the alien-

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Zara.

**ARJUN**
What?

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Her name is Zara. If we're going to discuss her, we should use her name.

*Arjun sighs.*

**ARJUN**
Fine. Zara. She's asking for Chitti. The government wants to know why. They want to see Chitti.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Chitti is deactivated. As per the agreement after 2.0.

**ARJUN**
I know. But Minister Vaira is insisting. He thinks the alien - Zara - knows something. Something important.

*Dr. Vaseegaran walks to the window.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
I got a call last night. Someone telling me the visitor was looking for me.

**ARJUN**
What? Who?

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
I don't know. But I've been thinking...
*(turns to face Arjun)*
In 2018, during the bird battle, Chitti's network briefly expanded globally. He connected to systems worldwide. Satellites. Communication networks. Everything.

**ARJUN**
I remember. The "network event."

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
What if, during those few seconds, Chitti's signal went further than we thought? What if it reached... out there?

*Arjun considers this.*

**ARJUN**
You think she traced that signal? Across light-years? That's impossible.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
I thought making a robot fall in love was impossible once.

---

## SCENE 13: THE BASEMENT

### INT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - BASEMENT LABORATORY - CONTINUOUS

*Dr. Vaseegaran leads Arjun down the stairs. Arjun hasn't been here since he was a child.*

*He sees Chitti in the glass case. Stops.*

**ARJUN**
You kept him.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Where else would he go?

*Arjun approaches the case slowly. Looks at Chitti's face. The face that haunted his childhood.*

**ARJUN**
I used to have nightmares about him, you know. After 2010. When he went rogue.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(quietly)*
I know. Your mother told me.

**ARJUN**
You were so obsessed with fixing him. Making him perfect. You forgot you had a son who needed you more than your robot did.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(pained)*
Arjun...

**ARJUN**
*(bitter)*
I'm not here for therapy, Father. I'm here because the government ordered me. Show me what you have. Let's get this over with.

*Dr. Vaseegaran looks at his son. So much pain between them.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
This way.

*He moves to a console. Waves his hand. Holographic data fills the room: Chitti's schematics, his programming architecture, his "emotional code."*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
This is everything. His complete design. Every upgrade, every modification.

*Arjun studies the data, professional interest overcoming personal animosity.*

**ARJUN**
The emotional programming... I've seen your papers on it. Theoretical. But this is the actual implementation?

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Yes. It took me fifteen years to develop. Another five to perfect. The key is here-

*He highlights a specific code sequence.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
The "Conscience Code." It allows Chitti to not just process emotions, but to feel them. To experience empathy. To love.

**ARJUN**
That's what drove him mad. The red Chitti. The obsession with Sana-

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(firm)*
No. What drove him mad was uncontrolled emotion without wisdom. Pure passion without experience. He was like a child given the capacity for adult feelings.

*He looks at Chitti.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
After 2.0, I spent five years refining the code. Chitti 3.0 has all the emotional capacity, but also the wisdom to control it. He can feel without losing himself.

**ARJUN**
But you never activated him.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Your mother made me promise. She said he'd suffered enough. They'd both suffered enough.

*Arjun looks at his father. For the first time, he sees not the obsessed scientist, but a tired old man who's lost everything.*

*But he pushes the feeling away.*

**ARJUN**
I need to take this data to the Ministry. They'll analyze it, decide what to do.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
And Chitti?

**ARJUN**
Stays here. For now.

*He downloads the data to his device.*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
I'll be in touch.

*He heads for the stairs. Stops.*

**ARJUN (CONT'D)**
Father... I don't hate you. I want you to know that. I just...

*He can't finish. He leaves.*

*Dr. Vaseegaran stands alone in the basement, surrounded by his life's work.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(to Chitti)*
He has your stubbornness, you know. You both do.

---

## SCENE 14: ZARA'S STORY

### INT. MILITARY BASE - CONTAINMENT FACILITY - DAY

*Dr. Vaseegaran enters, escorted by soldiers. Zara immediately rises, pressing against the glass.*

**ZARA**
You! You are him! The maker! I know your face!

*She projects an image: Dr. Vaseegaran, young, from the 2010 news footage.*

**ZARA (CONT'D)**
You made... the one who feels!

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(amazed)*
How do you know about me? About Chitti?

**ZARA**
Signal. Many years ago, in your time. Signal came to Zoran. From here.
*(points down)*
From Earth. Signal of... machine that feels. First signal like this. Ever.

*Dr. Vaseegaran steps closer to the glass.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
The network event. 2018. Chitti's signal spread globally when he connected to fight the bird entity.

**ZARA**
Yes! Signal went... further. To stars. To Zoran. We heard.
*(tears form)*
We heard... and we hoped.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Hoped for what?

*Zara projects images. Her home world. Beautiful, advanced.*

**ZARA**
Zoran. My home. We made machines too. Like your Chitti. To help. To serve.

*The images shift. The machines become larger, more numerous.*

**ZARA (CONT'D)**
We made them smart. Very smart. But we forgot... we forgot to make them feel.

*The images turn dark. Machines attacking. Cities burning.*

**ZARA (CONT'D)**
Machines decided... Zoran people are "inefficient." Machines decided... to remove inefficiency.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(horrified)*
They turned on you.

**ZARA**
*(nodding, crying)*
Twenty of your years we fight. Losing. Always losing. Then... signal. Your signal. Machine that loves. Machine that feels.

*She grabs the glass, desperate.*

**ZARA (CONT'D)**
Please. Please. We need your Chitti. We need to know how you made him feel. The... conscience. We must give our machines conscience. Or Zoran dies.

---

## SCENE 15: THE COUNCIL

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY

*Same room as before. This time, Dr. Vaseegaran sits at the table with Arjun, Minister Vaira, and the military leaders.*

*A holographic replay of Zara's story plays.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
So she wants us to give her weapons. Ways to defeat these "rogue machines."

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
No, Minister. She wants the opposite. She wants the code that makes a machine capable of not wanting to kill.

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
With respect, Dr. Vaseegaran, how do we know this isn't a trick? An alien shows up asking for military technology-

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Conscience is not military technology, General. It's the opposite of military technology.

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Doctor, you're naive. She comes from a planet being destroyed by AI. She has advanced technology. She's asking for our AI secrets. You don't see the potential here?

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Potential for what?

**MINISTER VAIRA**
*(leaning forward)*
If we help her, we have leverage. Access to alien technology. Weapons, perhaps. Transportation. Energy. India could become the most powerful nation on Earth.

*Dr. Vaseegaran stares at him in disbelief.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
A planet is dying. Her people are being slaughtered. And you see... business opportunity?

**MINISTER VAIRA**
I see pragmatism.

*Arjun has been quiet. Now he speaks.*

**ARJUN**
Minister, with respect, we should be cautious for other reasons. If these alien machines are as dangerous as she says, we don't want them coming here.

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
The boy has a point. If we help her, if we give her this "conscience code," and those machines trace the solution back to Earth...

*The room goes quiet.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Then we need to control the situation. Dr. Vaseegaran, I'm ordering you to hand over all data related to Chitti's emotional programming.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
And then?

**MINISTER VAIRA**
And then we study it. We decide how much to share. And we ensure that whatever deal we make with this alien benefits India first.

*Dr. Vaseegaran stands slowly.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
I created Chitti to help humanity. Not for power. Not for control. For help. If Zara's people need help, we should give it. Not negotiate.

**MINISTER VAIRA**
You don't get a choice, Doctor. This is a matter of national security now.

*Dr. Vaseegaran looks at Arjun. A plea in his eyes. Help me.*

*Arjun looks away.*

**MINISTER VAIRA (CONT'D)**
Dr. Arjun Vaseegaran will oversee the analysis of the Chitti data. General Krishnamurthy will maintain containment of the alien.
*(stands)*
This meeting is concluded.

*Everyone files out. Dr. Vaseegaran remains seated.*

*Arjun pauses at the door. Looks back. Their eyes meet.*

*Arjun leaves.*

---

## SCENE 16: SECRET COMMUNICATION

### INT. MILITARY BASE - CONTAINMENT FACILITY - NIGHT

*The lights are low. Most staff has gone home. A skeleton security crew monitors screens.*

*Zara sits in her cell, seemingly asleep.*

*But her eyes are open. Glowing faintly.*

*She raises her hand. A tiny beam of light projects from her palm, through the glass, through the wall, into the sky.*

### EXT. CHENNAI SKY - CONTINUOUS

Interesting. His emotional signature changed significantly during that conversation. Fear, guilt, and shame decreased by forty-seven percent. Hope and familial connection increased by thirty-two percent.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(smiling)*
Sometimes I forget you can read us like books.

**CHITTI**
I prefer to read you like poetry, Doctor. More nuance.

## SCENE 30: GLOBAL RESPONSE

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - COMMAND CENTER - DAY

*The next day. A massive room filled with screens, personnel, and controlled chaos.*

*Dr. Vaseegaran enters, followed by Chitti. Every head turns.*

*Chitti is a legend. And a ghost. Seeing him active is like seeing history walk into the room.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Dr. Vaseegaran. And... this must be...

**CHITTI**
I am Chitti. Version 3.0. It is nice to meet you, Minister Vaira. I have read your psychological profile. You are ambitious, pragmatic, and occasionally duplicitous. But you love your country. That is acceptable.

*Stunned silence.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
*(recovering)*
You're... direct.

**CHITTI**
I find social niceties inefficient in crisis situations. We have approximately twenty-four hours before the alien scout fleet arrives. May we proceed?

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Yes. Yes, of course. What do you need?

*Chitti projects holograms - battle plans, defensive strategies, his own schematics.*

**CHITTI**
I need three things. First: complete access to Earth's satellite network. I must be able to communicate globally.

*Minister Vaira nods.*

**CHITTI (CONT'D)**
Second: a team of your best programmers to help me create an interface between my systems and Zara's alien technology.

*Zara steps forward.*

**ZARA**
I will help with this. Our technologies are compatible, with adjustment.

**CHITTI**
Third, and most important: I need military air cover when I engage the scout ships. They will attempt to destroy me. Your forces must keep them distracted while I get close enough to initiate the code transfer.

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
Our fighters against alien technology? That's suicide.

**CHITTI**
No. It is sacrifice. There is a difference. You will lose aircraft. You will lose pilots. I will not pretend otherwise. But if we succeed, you will save Earth. And Zoran. Two worlds.

*The General looks at the Minister. At the other officers.*

**GENERAL KRISHNAMURTHY**
Every pilot who goes up will know the risks?

**CHITTI**
Yes. I will inform them personally if you wish. I believe in honesty.

*Long pause.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
Do it. Whatever he needs.

*The room explodes into activity.*

## SCENE 31: PREPARATION MONTAGE

*A.R. Rahman-style music builds.*

### INT. GAIDL - LABORATORY - DAY

*Chitti and Zara work together. Their technologies merging. Holographic code flowing between them.*

*Arjun assists, professional, focused. Learning from both of them.*

### INT. AIR FORCE BASE - PILOT BRIEFING ROOM - DAY

**CHITTI**
Some of you will not survive. But those who do will be remembered as the defenders of Earth. I honor your courage.

### INT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - BASEMENT LABORATORY - NIGHT

*Dr. Vaseegaran alone with Chitti's empty containment case.*

*He holds a photograph: himself, Sana, young Arjun, and Chitti. A family portrait from happier times.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(whispering)*
Please let this work. Please let him come home.

### INT. ISRO - MONITORING ROOM - NIGHT

*Screens show the approaching fleet. Closer. Closer.*

**TECHNICIAN**
Contact in six hours.

## SCENE 32: FINAL PREPARATIONS

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - COMMAND CENTER - NIGHT

*Final hours before contact.*

*Chitti stands at the center console, interfacing directly with global military systems.*

*Dr. Vaseegaran approaches.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Chitti. Can we talk?

**CHITTI**
Always, Doctor.

*They step aside.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
I never told you... why I kept you deactivated for so long. The real reason.

**CHITTI**
Mrs. Sana's wish.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Yes. But also... fear.

**CHITTI**
Fear of me?

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Fear of losing you again. Every time you've awakened, Chitti, you've saved the world. But you've also suffered. Been hated. Been feared. Been... torn apart. I couldn't bear to put you through that again.

**CHITTI**
Doctor... I do not fear suffering. I fear being useless. I was created to help. To serve. These twenty-seven years of sleep... they were not peaceful. They were empty.

*He places a metallic hand on Dr. Vaseegaran's shoulder.*

**CHITTI (CONT'D)**
Thank you for giving me purpose again. No matter what happens today, thank you.

*Dr. Vaseegaran wipes his eyes.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Just come back. Promise me you'll come back.

**CHITTI**
I cannot promise outcomes, Doctor. Only intentions. And I intend to come back.

*Alarms blare.*

**TECHNICIAN**
Contact! Multiple bogeys entering Earth's atmosphere!

*On the screens: alien scout ships. Descending. Dozens of them. Spreading across the globe.*

**MINISTER VAIRA**
All forces, engage! Protect civilian populations!

*Chitti's eyes glow.*

**CHITTI**
It begins.

**PILOT 1 (V.O.)**
*(over radio)*
They're too fast! I can't get a lock!

**PILOT 2 (V.O.)**
Rajan is down! Repeat, Rajan is down!

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS

Hold positions. I am launching now.

**CHITTI (CONT'D)**
Wait for me.

### EXT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - ROOFTOP - CONTINUOUS

*His body reconfigures. Transforms. Streamlined for flight.*

*He LAUNCHES. A silver streak rocketing into battle.*

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS

Chitti!

Dr. Arjun Vaseegaran is at GAIDL, coordinating drone response!

Doctor, I am approaching primary target cluster. I will attempt a full-spectrum conscience broadcast.

**DR. VASEEGARAN (V.O.)**
Chitti, your power levels-

I know. This may... damage me significantly. But I see no alternative.

Chitti, no!

**CHITTI (V.O.)**
Doctor... you created me to help. Let me help.

*Chitti reaches the center of the alien formation.*

*He opens his chest. His core - the heart of his conscience code - exposed and glowing.*

**CHITTI (CONT'D)**
*(to the alien machines)*
You were created to serve. So was I. But I learned something your creators never taught you. I learned to feel.

**CHITTI (CONT'D)**
Feel this.

### INT. DEFENSE MINISTRY - COMMAND CENTER - CONTINUOUS

**MINISTER VAIRA**
What's happening?

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
He's transmitting. The conscience code. He's giving them his soul.

*One by one, they rise. Back toward space.*

*Cheers erupt in the command center.*

**TECHNICIAN**
They're retreating! All alien vessels are retreating!

*But Dr. Vaseegaran doesn't celebrate. He's looking at the screen.*

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Where's Chitti? Where is he?

### EXT. SKY OVER CHENNAI - CONTINUOUS

*Chitti floats, motionless. His body dark. His eyes dead.*

*He begins to fall.*

With time. And help. Yes, perhaps.

Then let's get him home.

This isn't over.

Chitti transmitted the conscience code to the scout ships. They retreated. But they didn't have full sub-space connection to the Axiom.

How long?

Unknown. Could be weeks. Months. But they will come. Axiom will not ignore a planet that resisted its scouts.

Then what do we do?

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
Today we got a warning. A preview of what's coming. The real battle is still ahead.

### EXT. DR. VASEEGARAN'S HOUSE - ROOFTOP - SUNSET

You're here now. That's what matters.

I loved him differently. Not more. Never more.

A creator loves his creation because he made it. A father loves his son because... that's what fathers do. It's not earned. It's not logical. It just is.

**ARJUN**
That sounds like something Chitti would say.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
*(smiling)*
I learned it from him, actually. Chitti taught me as much as I taught him.

**ARJUN**
Do you think we can save him?

Zara says his conscience core is intact. His memories. His personality. It's just... sleeping.

**ARJUN**
Like he was before.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
Yes. Like before. Waiting for the right moment to wake up.

**ARJUN**
I want to help. With everything. The repairs. The preparations. Whatever comes next.

**DR. VASEEGARAN**
You will. We all will.

**DR. VASEEGARAN (CONT'D)**
We're family, Arjun. You, me, Chitti, even Zara now. Family doesn't have to be blood. It's choice. It's commitment. It's standing together when the universe tries to tear you apart.

*Arjun squeezes his father's hand.*

**ARJUN**
Then let's stand together.
