# SWA Registration Guide for Robot 3: The Return

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Go to SWA Website
**URL:** https://swaindia.org/

### Step 2: Create Account / Sign Up
1. Click on "Sign Up" or "Register"
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name: **Suman Addanki** (or your legal name)
   - Email: Your email address
   - Phone: Your phone number
   - Address: Your full address

### Step 3: Choose Membership Type

For first-time/new writers, choose **"Fellow Membership"**:

| Membership Type | Who Can Apply | Duration | Approx. Fee |
|----------------|---------------|----------|-------------|
| **Fellow** | Anyone with passion for writing | 3 years | ~₹1,500-3,000 |
| Associate | Degree/Diploma in screenwriting | 3 years | ~₹3,000-5,000 |
| Regular | 2+ professional credits | Annual | ~₹3,500 |
| Life | Professional writers | Lifetime | ~₹18,000 |

**Recommended for you:** Fellow Membership (no prior credits required)

### Step 4: Pay Membership Fee
- Payment options: Credit/Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking
- GST (18%) will be added to fees

### Step 5: Wait for Membership Approval
- Takes 10-12 working days
- You'll receive membership number via email

### Step 6: Register Your Screenplay

Once membership is approved:

1. Log in to SWA portal
2. Go to "Script Registration"
3. Upload your screenplay:
   - **Title:** Robot 3: The Return (Part 1 & Part 2)
   - **Type:** Screenplay
   - **Language:** English (or Hindi if translated)
   - **Pages:** ~300 (both parts combined)

4. Pay script registration fee (~₹500-1,000 per script)

### Step 7: Receive Registration Certificate
- Digital certificate with unique registration number
- Valid proof of authorship with timestamp

## Important Notes

### What SWA Registration Protects:
✅ Your specific screenplay text and dialogue
✅ Original characters you created (Zara, Arjun, Axiom)
✅ Your unique plot elements and scenes
✅ Timestamp proving when you wrote it

### What SWA Registration Does NOT Protect:
❌ Dr. Vaseegaran character (belongs to original creators)
❌ Chitti character (belongs to original creators)
❌ "Robot/Enthiran" franchise name
❌ General story ideas or concepts

### Legal Standing:
- SWA registration is recognized by Indian film industry
- Can be used as evidence in disputes
- Recognized by Film Producers Guild
- Has dispute resolution committee

---

## Alternative: Copyright Office India

If you want additional protection:

**Copyright Office India**
- Website: https://copyright.gov.in/
- Fee: ₹500-2,000 per work
- Duration: Lifetime + 60 years
- Stronger legal protection than SWA

**Recommendation:** Do BOTH - SWA for industry recognition + Copyright Office for legal protection
